Hokkien Noodles

Description

Hokkien noodles is a dry noodle dish that is stir fried with a special sauce. It is a great, simple and quick dish to make, as any vegetables and meat of your liking can be added.

Ingredients

  • 500g/ 1 lb hokkien noodles, (fridge best)
  • 350g/ 12 oz chicken thighs (boneless, skinless), cut into lot of thin 0.5 x 3cm / 0.2 x 1.2"
  • 2 tbsp canola oil (or vegetable, cottonseed, peanut oil)
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 8 green onion stems, cut into 5cm / 2″ lengths, white part separated from green part
  • 1 red capsicum (bell peppers), cut into thin 0.5cm / 0.2″ strips
  • 3 heaped cups green cabbage, cut into thin 1 x 5cm / 0.5 x 2" strips

    • Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp Mirin or Chinese cooking wine
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil, toasted
  • 1/8 tsp white pepper

    • Optional Garnishes:

  • White sesame seeds
  • Finely sliced green onion
